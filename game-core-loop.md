# Game Core Loop - Into Life

## Overall Vision
"Into Life" is a realistic life simulation where the player starts at age 16 and must build their adult life through their own decisions in a shared 3D world.

## Core Loop (Daily Life)
1. **Wake up** in current living place (hostel, rented room, etc.)
2. **Manage basic needs** (eat, rest, hygiene - simple systems)
3. **Make choices** about how to spend the day:
   - Go look for a job
   - Attend university classes (if enrolled)
   - Study / prepare for exams
   - Explore the town / meet other players
   - Handle money, loans, bills
4. **Interact with the world** naturally (talk to NPCs, read notice boards, visit places)
5. **End the day** — sleep and save progress

## Key Principle
- The game **never makes choices** for the player.
- The game only gives **neutral advice** through signs, NPCs, or information boards.
- Player must discover and do everything themselves (find jobs, apply for university, pay rent, etc.).

## Starting Point (First Prototype)
- Graduation ceremony → Exit school → First small area
- No daily loop yet — just freedom to walk and explore

## Long-term Core Loop Goals
- Player manages time, money, energy, and relationships
- Natural meetings with other real players in the same world
- Life progression (age increases slowly, skills improve, etc.)
- Multiple paths (university graduate, worker, entrepreneur, etc.)

## Success Metric
Player should feel: "This is my life. I am making real choices."

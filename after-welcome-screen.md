# After Welcome Screen - First Area

## What Happens After the Black Screen
- After the text "Welcome Into Life......" fades out, the player appears in a small, quiet outdoor area just outside the school gate.
- This is the very first "free" area where the player can walk around and start making their own decisions.

## Desired Feeling
- Freedom mixed with slight uncertainty and excitement about adult life.
- The world feels alive but not overwhelming (small scope for first prototype).

## What the Player Should See (Simple Version)
- A quiet road or path leading away from the school.
- A few simple buildings or landmarks visible in the distance (placeholders):
  - A small hostel or apartment building
  - A notice board for jobs
  - An internet café sign
  - A bus stop or small park area
- Some basic environment: trees, grass, sky, maybe a few street lights or signs.
- No other players yet (single-player only for now).

## Player Controls
- First-person view (as established earlier).
- Free movement with WASD + mouse look.
- Ability to look around and explore at their own pace.
- No forced objectives — the player decides where to go.

## Important Rules for This Area
- The game gives only gentle advice (through signs, NPCs, or text).
- The player must discover things by themselves (find jobs, university, hostel, etc.).
- Keep this area small and simple for the first prototype.

## Development Notes
- Use basic 3D shapes and placeholder models at first.
- Focus on making the movement and first-person camera feel good.
- This area will later connect to a larger starter town.
- Future addition: possibility of seeing other real players walking in the distance (much later).

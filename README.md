# INTO LIFE

A solo 3D life simulation game where players start at age 16, just after finishing high school, and build their own adult life in a shared realistic world.

## Current First Prototype Goal (Very Small - Single Player Only)

**Graduation Ceremony Scene Flow:**

- The conference hall is round (circular/auditorium style).
- Chairs are arranged in rising tiers (lowest rows closest to the stage, higher rows going upward toward the back).
- There is a stage at the bottom/center with a large screen behind it that shows what is happening in the room.

**Player Actions During Ceremony:**
- Player starts seated in one of the chairs.
- When their name is called, the player stands up immediately with happiness and does a small jump/celebration.
- Then walks down the stairs/aisle toward the stage.
- On the stage: receives the diploma and takes a photo with the school principal and the people giving the diplomas.
- After receiving the diploma, the player walks out of the building.

**Exiting the School:**
- When the player goes out of the building, they **turn around** (in first-person view, not third-person).
- The player looks at the whole school building from bottom to the top through their own eyes.
- Then turns around again and walks toward the school gate.
- At the gate, the gate keeper says:  
  “Kid, you are finishing but for a better future you can attend a university or find a job.”
- The player then goes out of the school.
- After exiting, a blank black screen appears with the text:  
  **"Welcome Into Life......"**

**Advice at the Gate (additional text that appears):**
“For surviving a long time, you can attend university for studying or find a job. 
You can find job sectors on the map to see where you can ask for a job, or find the internet café for finding some universities to study and apply for scholarship, or visit them to pay for yourself if you haven’t been selected.”

**Desired Feeling:**
Freedom mixed with slight uncertainty and excitement about the future.

## Character Creation (For Later)
- When the player creates their account, they take a real photo of their face.
- The game will automatically generate a 3D character with the same face as the player.

## Development Rules
- We build slowly, one tiny piece at a time.
- No rushing. No multiplayer or big world yet.
- First focus: Make the graduation + school exit + gate moment feel good in single-player.
- Later we will add real choices, jobs, hostels, universities, and eventually other players.

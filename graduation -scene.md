# Graduation Scene - Detailed Description

## Hall Layout
- Round (circular) conference hall / auditorium style.
- Chairs arranged in rising tiers (like a stadium): lowest rows near the stage, higher rows going upward toward the back.
- Stage at the bottom/center with a large screen behind it that shows live view of the ceremony.

## Player Starting Position
- Player begins seated in one of the chairs in the audience.

## Ceremony Sequence
1. Player is sitting.
2. When their name is called, the player **stands up immediately with happiness** and does a small jump/celebration animation.
3. Player walks down the stairs/aisle toward the stage.
4. On the stage:
   - Receives the high school diploma.
   - Takes a photo with the school principal and the people giving the diplomas.
5. After the ceremony, player walks out of the hall and exits the school building.

## Exiting the School Building (First-Person Important Moment)
- When the player steps outside the building, they **turn around** (in first-person view, not third-person).
- The player looks up at the whole school building from bottom to the top through their own eyes.
- This moment should give a feeling of freedom mixed with slight uncertainty and excitement.
- Then the player turns around again and walks toward the school gate.

## At the School Gate
- Gate keeper speaks:  
  “Kid, you are finishing but for a better future you can attend a university or find a job.”
- Additional advice text appears:  
  “For surviving a long time, you can attend university for studying or find a job. 
  You can find job sectors on the map to see where you can ask for a job, or find the internet café for finding some universities to study and apply for scholarship, or visit them to pay for yourself if you haven’t been selected.”

## After Leaving the Gate
- Player walks out of the school area.
- Screen fades to black.
- Text appears in the center:  
  **"Welcome Into Life......"**

## Notes for Development
- Keep this first prototype very simple (basic movement, placeholder models, simple text UI).
- Focus on making the first-person turn-around moment to see the building feel meaningful.
- Character will later use a face generated from the player's real photo during account creation.

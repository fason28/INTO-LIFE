# School Gate Area - First Prototype

## Overview
This is the first real "open" area the player experiences after the graduation ceremony. It marks the true beginning of adult life in "Into Life".

## Sequence After Exiting the Building
1. Player turns around (first-person) and looks up at the entire school building from bottom to top.
2. Player turns back and walks toward the school gate.
3. At the gate, the gate keeper says:  
   “Kid, you are finishing but for a better future you can attend a university or find a job.”
4. Additional advice text appears on screen (neutral, not forcing any choice):
   “For surviving a long time, you can attend university for studying or find a job. 
   You can find job sectors on the map to see where you can ask for a job, or find the internet café for finding some universities to study and apply for scholarship, or visit them to pay for yourself if you haven’t been selected.”
5. Player walks past the gate and leaves the school grounds.
6. Screen fades to black.
7. Text appears: **"Welcome Into Life......"**

## What the Gate Area Should Feel Like
- Small, simple outdoor area (not a huge world yet).
- A school gate/fence with a small guard post or booth for the gate keeper.
- A quiet road or path leading away from the school.
- A few basic environmental details: trees, a signboard, maybe a bus stop or notice board in the distance (placeholders for now).
- Feeling: Freedom mixed with slight uncertainty and excitement about the future.

## Development Notes for First Prototype
- Use simple 3D shapes (boxes, planes) for the building, gate, and ground.
- First-person camera control is very important here.
- Gate keeper can be a simple static NPC with dialogue triggered when player gets close.
- No real choices or systems yet — just movement, looking around, and text/dialogue.
- After the black screen, we will decide what the player sees next (small town area?).

## Future Ideas (Much Later)
- The gate area could connect to a larger starter town with:
  - Job notice boards
  - Internet café
  - University offices
  - Hostels
- Other players might occasionally be visible walking around (much later).
